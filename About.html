About This Website

Developed by Srujana Damarla, MS in Information Science

Watching movies has always been one of the most opted means of entertainment. I personally enjoy watching movies and shows in my free time. I always depend on various online sources to decide which movie I should watch. These sources have always been helpful and would contain a variety of movies and shows divided into various categories. However, they usually have much more information than what I would need which would make me go through a lot of content before I reach my required information. So having this in mind I wanted to design a website that would satisfy my needs and respond to my concerns. Also, I would be more than glad to expand this website to meet the requirements of other movies lovers like me.
